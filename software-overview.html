Software Overview

dYNAMICO Software

Typically, project analyses contain thousands of elements and hundreds of load combinations, resulting in up to, or even over, a million design cases. Dynamico software automates these designs for each and every finite element for every single load combination.

By automating the design process, along with the producing a graphical visualisation of the results, structural engineers can quickly and easily determine required reinforcement (concrete) or where there are high utilisation ratios (concrete and steel).
