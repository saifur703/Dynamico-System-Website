About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionise structural engineering by embracing digital innovation to automate and optimise the design process, improving efficiency and productivity

Dynamico Systems gives structural engineers a more efficient way to design their structures that takes away the headaches of using traditional Black Box software. Dynamico Systems breaks the mould and adopts a completely transparent ‘Open Box’ approach that allows structural engineers to see exactly what is going on inside.

The structural engineers and analysts in our team leading the product development have years of experience working in industry and battling with existing analysis and design software. Our team understands the complexity and frustrations that engineers face, and so launched Dynamico Systems with a focus on transparency and trust.

“Use whichever software you need for the structural analysis, but let us take care of the design using fast and powerful automation that gives you consistency across all your projects, and that hides nothing, to make your validation a breeze!”

Our Team

We take pride in having a team of Chartered Engineers driving strategy and software development, with the backing of experienced technical experts focusing on the core product offering.

  • Chartered Engineers
  • Capital Project and Infrastructure Experts
  • Pioneers for technology in construction
  • Technical Experts

Our team have extensive experience in a wide range of industries, particularly in the nuclear sector which demands a heightened level of rigor and technical excellence. It is this experience that drives the passion we have for our product as we understand the need and impact it can have.
